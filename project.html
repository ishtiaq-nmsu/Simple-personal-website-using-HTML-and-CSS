higherstudyprep.com



Co-founder & content creator of higherstudyprep.com, an online educational platform

Higher Study Prep is a website to help students to make the journey easier and help them in every aspect of higher study. This website provides guidlines and tools for better preparation for standardized test. Students from different countries very often face problems to find a proper guidline to start their higher study journey. Our intention is to help those students. Many of the websites services are completely free for the students



reviewbangla.com



Co-founded reviewbangla.com, a review collecting, monitoring and analyzing platform

Review Bangla is a business review platform where people can post review in Bengali language. In USA people can use website like yelp.com to get an idea about diferent type of entities. However, in Bangladesh there is no such kind of website is available. Our idea was to build a bias free review platform so that the commuinity can be benifited. The unique selling point of this idea is people can post review in Bangla.
